Itinerary for Jaisalmer Jodhpur Tour Package

Day 1: Arrival in Jaisalmer

- Arrive at Jaisalmer and check into your hotel.

- Visit Jaisalmer Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage site.

- Explore the Patwon Ki Haveli and Salim Singh Ki Haveli.

- Evening visit to Sam Sand Dunes for a desert sunset.

Day 2: Jaisalmer Sightseeing

- Breakfast at the hotel.

- Visit the Kuldhara Village and Gadi Sagar Lake.

- Afternoon camel safari and cultural program.

- Dinner under the stars at the desert camp.

Day 3: Travel to Jodhpur

- Depart Jaisalmer after breakfast.

- Arrive in Jodhpur and check into your hotel.

- Visit the Mehrangarh Fort and Jaswant Thada.

- Evening stroll in the local market.

Day 4: Departure

- Breakfast at the hotel.

- Visit Umaid Bhawan Palace and Mandore Gardens.

- Depart for your onward journey.
