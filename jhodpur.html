Andaman Tour Itinerary - 7 Days

Arrive at Port Blair Airport, transfer to your hotel. After check-in, visit the famous Cellular Jail and attend the Light & Sound Show in the evening. Overnight stay at Port Blair.

Early morning ferry to Havelock Island. Enjoy your day at the famous Radhanagar Beach, known for its pristine beauty and clear waters. Relax or indulge in water activities. Overnight stay at Havelock.

Visit the stunning Elephant Beach in the morning, known for its coral reefs and vibrant marine life. Enjoy snorkeling, scuba diving, and other water sports. Return to Port Blair by evening ferry. Overnight stay at Port Blair.

After breakfast, visit Ross Island, once the administrative headquarters of the British in the Andamans. Explore the ruins and wildlife, then head to North Bay Island for water activities like snorkeling. Overnight stay at Port Blair.

Take a ferry to Neil Island. Visit the Natural Bridge, Bharatpur Beach, and Laxmanpur Beach. Enjoy the serene beauty and relax. Overnight stay at Neil Island.

Return to Havelock Island. Visit the picturesque Kalapathar Beach, known for its black rocks and clear waters. Spend your day relaxing and enjoying the scenic beauty. Overnight stay at Havelock.

After breakfast, check out from your hotel and transfer to Port Blair Airport for your onward journey, cherishing the wonderful memories of the Andaman Islands.
