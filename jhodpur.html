Jaisalmer 2 Days / 3 Night

Our driver will received at railway station or bus station and drop you at hotel where you can rest for 2-3 hours. After that in our half day sightseeing tour we visit with tour guide Jaisalmer fort, ancient jain temple, cannon on fort, raja ka mahal and visit 450 years old havelis ( patwa haveli ,nathmal haveli , salim singh ki haveli) vyas chattri and in evening gadishar lake where you can see laser light show. Overnight stay at hotel

After breakfast you will visit tanot mata temple in ac cab which is 130 km from jaisalmer which is situated at indo-pak border and after you’ll visit longevala- battle field of india- pak war. By the 5-6 pm you will reach sam desert where desert camp is already booked they will received you with traditional welcome after that you will go for came ride for 30-40 mins, they will take you at middle of the desert and sunset point where you can take photographs and enjoy sunset From 7.30 pm you will enjoy cultural music programs and kalbeliya dance with buffet dinner and stay overnight in tent

After breakfast you will check out from desert camp and visit kuldhara haunted village khabha fort, jaisari lake and bada bagh after that our driver will drop you at railway station or bus stand . Note- For guests who booked their return train or flight from jodhpur tell us in advance because jodhpur is 5-6 hours ride from jaisalmer. Thank you Khama Ghani
